Burger Recipe

A very healthy AND flavorful burger with a little extra crunch.

For 6 servings:

  • 2 pounds extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 ounce dry onion soup mix
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 2 tsp hot pepper sauce
  • 2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup rolled oats

Recipe:

  1. Step 1: Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Step 2: In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion soup mix, egg, hot sauce and oats. Shape into 6 patties.
  3. Step 3:Grill patties over medium high heat for 10 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness.
